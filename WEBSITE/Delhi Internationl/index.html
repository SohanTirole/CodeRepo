About Us

With a broader vision, our aim is to enhance every child’s personality by accessing his/her scholastic and co scholastic development. DIS is a steadfast advocate of environment issues. We inculcate moral values in students. We empower the students with knowledge and make them evolve as complete human beings.



DIS - 23 School Tour

Welcome to Delhi International School, Dwarka Sector 23 - a place where learning is joyful, creativity flourishes, and every child discovers their true potential. From the moment you step through our gates, you feel the warmth, discipline, and vibrant energy that define our school culture. Our classrooms and multipurpose auditorium are equipped with interactive smart boards, digital learning tools, and flexible seating that encourage collaboration and curiosity. Our well-equipped labs and libraries are spaces that encourage immersive learning imparting knowledge retention, engagement, and the ability to apply skills in the real world. Nurturing both mind and body, DIS has world-class sports facilities helping students build confidence, leadership, and team spirit offering cricket, basketball, football, futsal, skating, yoga, taekwondo and many more sports. Besides this, our performing art program encourages students to explore music, dance, drama, and fine arts- fostering expression, confidence, and emotional balance. Every student at DIS experiences learning that extends beyond the classroom — through a world driven by technology, we empower our learners through digital literacy, coding, and project-based learning - integrating innovation across all subjects. Safety and well-being are our highest priorities. With 24x7 CCTV monitoring, RFID entries, GPS-enabled transport, apt medical facilities and clean washrooms, we ensure every child feels secure, supported, and cared for. At Delhi International School, we don’t just prepare students for assessments — we prepare them for life. Rooted in values, driven by innovation, and committed to excellence thereby “Creating Global Heads with Hearts”
